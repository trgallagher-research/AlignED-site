Data Contents

Summary Data (Direct Download)

Two summary datasets are available:

  • Composite scores: Educational Alignment Index (EAI) composite, Neuromyths, Scenarios, CDPK, SEND for 21 models
  • ACARA scores: Accuracy and consistency on comparative judgement for 12 models

Full Dataset (Via OSF)

The complete dataset includes:

  • Item-level responses for all benchmarks
  • Raw model outputs and reasoning
  • Judge scoring with explanations (for scenarios)
  • Temperature robustness data (T=0, 0.5, 1.0)
  • Prompt sensitivity results (4 framings)
  • Confidence probe responses
  • Token usage statistics

Requesting Full Dataset Access

The full dataset is hosted on the Open Science Framework (OSF). To request access:

  1. Email your request to the address below
  2. Include your intended use (research, policy analysis, development, etc.)
  3. Provide your institutional affiliation (if applicable)

Access is typically granted within 2-3 business days for legitimate research and policy purposes.

Contact for data access: aligned.benchmark [at] gmail.com

Citation

If you use AlignED data in your work, please cite:

AlignED Benchmark (2026). Transparent benchmarks for AI in education. https://trgallagher-research.github.io/AlignED-site/

For academic publications, a formal citation will be available once the accompanying paper is published.

Terms of Use

AlignED data is provided for research, policy analysis, and educational purposes. When using the data:

  • Cite AlignED appropriately
  • Do not misrepresent scores as "certification" or "approval"
  • Acknowledge limitations noted in methodology
  • Contact us if you plan to redistribute modified versions
