What is educational alignment?

When AI is used in classrooms, it needs to know what teachers know — not just facts, but which beliefs about learning are wrong, how to handle real classroom problems, and what good teaching actually looks like. That's educational alignment.

Who should care: Teachers choosing AI tools, school leaders making procurement decisions, policymakers writing AI guidelines, EdTech companies selecting which AI model powers their tools — a decision too often based only on cost and speed, when educational alignment should also matter — and developers building educational AI.
