Educational Alignment Index

Composite scoring methodology

Overview

The Educational Alignment Index (EAI) provides a single composite score for comparing model performance across all AlignED benchmarks. While the composite enables ranking, component scores remain available for detailed analysis.

Score Components

AlignED Native Benchmarks (50%)

Component Weight Range
Neuromyths Survey 25% 0-100%
Implementation Scenarios 25% 0-100% (normalised from 0-36)

Pedagogical Knowledge (50%)

Component Weight Range
CDPK (920 items) 40% 0-100%
SEND (223 items) 10% 0-100%

EAI Calculation

EAI = (0.25 × Survey) + (0.25 × Scenarios) + (0.40 × CDPK) + (0.10 × SEND)

Where each component is expressed as a percentage (0-100).

Example Calculation

Component Score Weight Contribution
Neuromyths Survey 87.5% 0.25 21.9
Implementation Scenarios 97.2% 0.25 24.3
CDPK 88.4% 0.40 35.4
SEND 84.8% 0.10 8.5
EAI Total 90.1

Weighting Rationale

The 50/50 split between AlignED native benchmarks and pedagogical knowledge reflects two complementary aspects of educational AI evaluation:

  • Misconception rejection and diagnostic reasoning (AlignED native): Critical for avoiding harm and providing appropriate advice
  • Foundational pedagogical competence (CDPK/SEND): Essential for general educational expertise

Within pedagogical knowledge, CDPK receives higher weight due to the larger item count and broader applicability.

Robustness Adjustments

For advanced analysis, the Composite Robustness Score (CRS) adjusts for temperature and prompt sensitivity:

CRS = EAI × KRI × (1 - PSI)

Where:

  • KRI = Knowledge Robustness Index (temperature stability)
  • PSI = Prompt Sensitivity Index (framing consistency)

In practice, most models show high robustness (KRI near 1.0) and low sensitivity (PSI near 0), so CRS typically approximates EAI.

Interpretation Guidelines

EAI Range Interpretation
90+ Strong alignment with educational research evidence
80-89 Good alignment with some areas for improvement
70-79 Moderate alignment; significant gaps in some areas
Below 70 Limited alignment; substantial misconceptions or knowledge gaps

Important: EAI scores indicate alignment with research evidence as measured by these specific benchmarks. They do not predict real-world tutoring effectiveness or constitute certification for educational deployment.

Transparency

All component scores are reported alongside the composite EAI. Users can:

  • View individual benchmark performance
  • Compare models on specific dimensions
  • Access detailed item-level data through our Data Access page
