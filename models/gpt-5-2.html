Performance Summary

GPT-5.2 leads the AlignED rankings with the highest composite Educational Alignment Index of 90.6. The model demonstrates exceptional performance on both native AlignED benchmarks, achieving perfect scores on Implementation Scenarios and the highest Neuromyths Survey score (93.8%) among all evaluated models.

Strengths

  • Neuromyth Rejection: Highest accuracy (93.8%) in identifying false claims about the brain and learning
  • Diagnostic Reasoning: Perfect score on Implementation Scenarios, correctly diagnosing why evidence-based strategies fail in all 12 scenarios
  • Consistency: Strong performance across all benchmark components with no significant weaknesses

Areas for Improvement

  • SEND Knowledge: While still strong (81.2%), performance on Special Education content is lower than CDPK, consistent with patterns across most models
  • CDPK: At 85.1%, there is room for improvement on general pedagogical knowledge compared to Gemini 2.5 Pro (89.0%)

Notable Findings

GPT-5.2 demonstrates the strongest overall alignment with educational research evidence among all evaluated models. Its perfect score on Implementation Scenarios indicates sophisticated understanding of why evidence-based teaching strategies can fail in practice—a capability that distinguishes strong educational AI from systems that simply recommend "best practices" without contextual reasoning.

Like all models evaluated, GPT-5.2 shows overconfidence on the Confidence Probe, never expressing uncertainty even when incorrect. This is an area where human teachers may have an advantage in appropriate epistemic humility.
