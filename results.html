Key Findings

Top Performers

GPT-5.2 and GPT-5 lead the rankings, followed closely by Claude 4.5 Opus with extended thinking. The top models achieve 90%+ EAI scores, substantially above the ~50% human teacher baseline on neuromyth rejection.

Provider Patterns

Both Anthropic and OpenAI have models in the top 10. Google's Gemini 2.5 Pro shows strong pedagogical knowledge (89% CDPK), the highest in that category.

Extended Thinking Benefits

Models with extended thinking capabilities (Claude 4.5 Opus/Sonnet with Thinking) generally outperform their non-thinking counterparts, particularly on diagnostic scenarios.

Model Size Matters

Smaller models (Haiku, Mini variants) consistently score lower than their larger counterparts, with Claude 3 Haiku at the bottom with 59.5% EAI.

Understanding the Scores

The Educational Alignment Index combines four components:

  • Neuromyths Survey (25%): Rejection of brain-based misconceptions
  • Implementation Scenarios (25%): Diagnostic reasoning about why strategies fail
  • CDPK (40%): General pedagogical knowledge
  • SEND (10%): Special education knowledge

See our scoring methodology for complete details.

Note: These scores indicate alignment with research evidence as measured by these benchmarks. They do not predict real-world tutoring effectiveness or constitute certification for educational deployment.
